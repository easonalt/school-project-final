Valberry

Material

Valberries grow in groups of four berries on a single stem. They are found in the wild exclusively in Stormbearer Mountains and Stormbearer Point.

About

Description A plump and translucent berry that has a fragrant smell and a sweet, refreshing taste. In the past, the storm watchers' only solace was the sweetness of this fruit and hope for the city's peace.
How to obtain
  1. Bought from shops
  2. Found in the wild around Stormbearer Mountains and Stormbearer Point
  3. Gardening
