Qingxin

Material

Qingxin grow on the mountaintops and peaks throughout Liyue; many can notably be found in Minlin and Wuwang Hill.

About

Description A translucent white flower that only grows on the highest stone peaks. It eschews the warmth and moisture of the plains to gaze out afar from the solitary mountaintops.
How to obtain
  1. Bought from shops
  2. Found in the heights of Liyue, especially Minlin and Wuwang Hill
  3. Gardening
