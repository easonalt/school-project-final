Dango Milk

Consumable - Food

Dango Milk is a food item that the player can purchase from Tomoki in Inazuma City for 1,500 Mora (max 2 per day). This item is only available after completing Act I of Raiden Shogun's Story Quest, Reflections of Mortality.
Dango Milk restores 26% of Max HP to the selected character and regenerates 570 HP every 5 seconds for 30 seconds. Like most foods, this cannot target other players' characters in Co-Op Mode.
