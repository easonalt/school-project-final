Cream Stew

Consumable - Food

Cream Stew is a food item that the player can cook. The recipe is obtainable from Good Hunter for 2,500 Mora after reaching Adventure Rank 25.
Depending on the quality, Cream Stew decreases Stamina depleted by sprinting for all party members by 15/20/25% for 900 seconds. Like most foods, this has no effect for other players in Co-Op Mode.
When Barbara cooks Cream Stew, there is a chance Spicy Stew will be created instead.

Statistics

Description A meat and vegetable stew. The thick juices taste great with the tender meat and vegetables.
Effect Decreases all party members' sprinting Stamina consumption by 20% for 900s.
How to obtain
  1. Cooked
  2. Bought from shops

Trivia

Cream stew (Japanese: クリームシチュー kuriimu shichuu) is a type of soup in youshoku (lit. "overseas dish"), or Japanese cuisine based on European dishes.
